Before you throw away hundreds of dollars on specialized cleaners, open your pantry. Baking soda is the unsung hero of home cleaning. It's cheap, non-toxic, and incredibly effective.

## 1. Unclog Drains Instantly

Pour a cup of baking soda down the drain, followed by a cup of vinegar. Wait 5 minutes, then flush with boiling water.

## 2. Brighten White Laundry

Add half a cup to your laundry load to make whites brighter and colors more vibrant.

## 3. Clean Your Oven

Make a paste with water and baking soda. Apply it to the inside of your oven and let it sit overnight. Wipe clean in the morning.



## 4. Deodorize Carpets

Sprinkle baking soda on your carpets, let sit for 15 minutes, then vacuum. It absorbs odors better than expensive sprays.
