### The Original Recipe (Expanded)

To recreate the original baked feta pasta recipe, you'll need only a handful of ingredients. As with any dish, experimenting with flavors and adding personal touches will help you create your perfect version. Here's the fundamental recipe to get you started:

*   8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice
*   1/2 cup (115g) crumbled feta cheese
*   3 cloves garlic, minced
*   1 tablespoon lemon zest
*   2 tablespoons olive oil
*   Salt and pepper to taste
*   Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese for an added layer of flavor (not included in the original recipe)

### Instructions (Expanded)

**Step 1: Preheat Your Oven**
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

**Step 2: Cook the Pasta**
Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

**Step 3: Mix the Cheese Sauce**
In a medium bowl, combine crumbled feta cheese, minced garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

**Step 4: Assemble the Dish**
In a large baking dish, combine cooked pasta, cheese sauce, and optional grated Parmesan cheese (not included in the original recipe). Mix well to coat the pasta evenly.

**Step 5: Bake Until Golden**
Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the sauce is bubbly.

### Frequently Asked Questions (Expanded)

#### What is baked feta pasta?
Baked feta pasta is a Mediterranean-inspired dish made with pasta, crumbled feta cheese, garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil. It's a simple, delicious recipe that's perfect for home cooks of all levels.

#### How do I make baked feta pasta?
To make baked feta pasta, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and cook pasta according to package instructions. Mix crumbled feta cheese with minced garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil, then assemble the dish in a baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

#### Can I customize baked feta pasta?
Absolutely! Baked feta pasta is a versatile recipe that can be customized to suit your tastes. Try adding other ingredients like cherry tomatoes, spinach, or grilled chicken to make it your own.

#### What should I serve with baked feta pasta?
Baked feta pasta pairs well with a fresh salad, crusty bread, and a glass of white wine. For a complete meal, consider serving it alongside grilled shrimp or vegetables.

#### Can I prepare baked feta pasta ahead of time?
While baking the pasta just before serving is ideal to maintain its texture, you can prepare the cheese mixture in advance. Simply combine crumbled feta cheese, minced garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil and refrigerate until ready to use. When it's time to cook, simply mix the cheese sauce with cooked pasta and bake as instructed.
