### The Rise of Baked Feta Pasta

For those who may not be familiar, baked feta pasta is a Mediterranean-inspired dish that consists of **pasta, crumbled feta cheese, garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil**. What sets this recipe apart from others is the use of **baking the entire dish in the oven**, which creates a creamy, cheesy sauce infused with the flavors of the Mediterranean.

### The Original Recipe

So, how did this humble pasta dish become a sensation in Finland? According to interviews with Finnish food bloggers and enthusiasts, **the original recipe was born out of necessity**. In 2020, a group of Finnish home cooks discovered that they could use leftover feta cheese to create a delicious, filling pasta dish. Word quickly spread, and soon, the recipe was being shared on social media platforms and cooking forums.

Here's the original recipe for baked feta pasta:

*   8 oz (225g) pasta of your choice
*   1/2 cup (115g) crumbled feta cheese
*   3 cloves garlic, minced
*   1 tablespoon lemon zest
*   2 tablespoons olive oil
*   Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions

**Step 1: Preheat Your Oven**
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

**Step 2: Cook the Pasta**
Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

**Step 3: Mix the Cheese Sauce**
In a medium bowl, combine crumbled feta cheese, minced garlic, lemon zest, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

**Step 4: Assemble the Dish**
In a large baking dish, combine cooked pasta, cheese sauce, and additional grated Parmesan cheese (optional). Mix well to coat the pasta evenly.

**Step 5: Bake Until Golden**
Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the sauce is bubbly.

### Why This Recipe Works
