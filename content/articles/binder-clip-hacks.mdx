**10 Ways a Binder Clip Saves the Day — Number 7 Is Genius**

Are you tired of using expensive tools to solve everyday problems? Think again! The humble binder clip is often overlooked, but it's actually a versatile and resourceful ally in your daily life.

1. **Cable Management**: Use a binder clip to manage cables and keep them organized. Simply clip the excess cable ends together, and you'll never have to deal with tangled cords again.

2. **Temporary Fix**: A binder clip can be used as a temporary fix for broken zippers or broken locks. It's not a permanent solution, but it can get you out of a sticky situation in an emergency.

3. **Plant Markers**: Use a binder clip to label your plants. Simply attach the clip to the plant pot and write the plant's name on the clip.

4. **DIY Tool Holder**: Attach a binder clip to a pegboard or a wall to create a makeshift tool holder. It's perfect for small workshops, garages, or offices.

5. **Packing Organizer**: Use a binder clip to organize your packing materials when moving. You can use it to attach tags to boxes or keep them in order.

6. **Hanger for Accessories**: Attach a binder clip to the inside of a closet door to hang belts, hats, or bags.

7. **Custom Desk Organizer**: Use a binder clip to create a custom desk organizer. Simply attach clips to a wooden ruler or a cardboard strip to hold pens, pencils, and paper clips in place.

8. **Cordless Screwdriver Storage**: Attach a binder clip to the side of a cordless screwdriver to store the battery when not in use.

9. **Bike Chain Adjustment Tool**: Use a binder clip to create an adjustable tool for bike chain adjustments. Simply attach the clip to the bike's chain and adjust it to the desired length.

10. **Emergency Phone Holder**: Attach a binder clip to a phone stand or a tablet holder to create a makeshift emergency phone holder during natural disasters or power outages.

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### What types of materials can I use a binder clip for?

Binder clips are made from metal and plastic, making them versatile and suitable for various tasks. You can use them with most materials, including paper, fabric, and even wire.

#### Can I use a binder clip as a permanent fix for something?

While binder clips can be used as temporary fixes, they're not designed to be permanent solutions. They can corrode or break over time, so it's essential to use them sparingly and find more permanent solutions whenever possible.

#### How do I clean a binder clip?

To clean a binder clip, simply wipe it with a damp cloth and dry it thoroughly. You can also use a mixture of baking soda and water to remove stubborn stains or corrosion.
