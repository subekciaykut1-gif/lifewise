Don't throw away that brine! Pickle juice is packed with electrolytes and vinegar, making it a surprisingly powerful health drink.

## The Electrolyte Powerhouse

Pickle juice contains significant amounts of sodium and potassium, which are lost during exercise. It's better than water for rehydration.

## Instant Cramp Relief

Research shows that pickle juice can relieve muscle cramps in as little as 85 seconds. The vinegar triggers a reflex that stops the cramping.



## Blood Sugar Control

The vinegar in pickle juice may help improve insulin sensitivity and lower blood sugar levels after meals.

So next time you finish a jar of pickles, save the juice!
